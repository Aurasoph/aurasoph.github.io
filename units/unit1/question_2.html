Iron in the asteroid 16 Psyche is said to be valued at 8973 quadrillion euros EUR, where one quadrillion =1015.

James believes the asteroid is approximately spherical with radius 113 km. He uses this information to estimate its volume.

a.

Write down the value of the iron in the form a×10k where 1≤a<10 , k∈ℤ.

b.

Calculate James’s estimate of its volume, in km3.

c.

The actual volume of the asteroid is found to be 6.074×106 km3.

Find the percentage error in James’s estimate of the volume.
